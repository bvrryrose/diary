About Me

A Journey Rooted in Purpose

In 2026, Aizza Nawaz stepped into the halls of Gajju Khan Medical College & Hospital — the beginning of a journey she has spent years preparing her heart for. She is a first-year MBBS student whose path to medicine was never about prestige alone, but about answering a calling.

Her deepest conviction is that the fragmented, inequitable state of global healthcare is a wound that needs healing from within the system. She is building herself into that healer — methodically, fiercely, tenderly. Her long-term vision? To establish a charity that delivers world-class medical care to communities that have been overlooked and underserved.
